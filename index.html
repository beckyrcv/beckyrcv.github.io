(Becky) Vandewalle earned her Bachelors degree in Greek and Roman Studies from Rhodes College in 2012. After completing a Post-Baccalaureate program in Classics at the University of Pennsylvania from 2012-2014, Becky earned her MSc in GIS and Archaeology from the University of Edinburgh in 2015. After a year working in Sunnyvale, California, Becky moved to Urbana, IL to study Geography & GIS at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.
