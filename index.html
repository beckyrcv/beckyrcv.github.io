About

I am a Geography PhD student in the CyberInfrastructure & Geospatial Information Lab, which is part of the CyberGIS Center at the University of Illinois Urbana Champaign. I currently study under Dr. Shaowen Wang. My research focuses on adapting spatial agent-based models to run on high performance computing resources. For more information, check out my "about" page and my my "research" page.

CyberGIS-ABM

I'm currently working on CyberGIS-ABM, a spatial agent-based model framework designed to tackle large scale simulations. check out my "research" page for more information.
