About

I earned my Bachelors degree in Greek and Roman Studies from Rhodes College in 2012. After completing a Post-Baccalaureate program in Classics at the University of Pennsylvania, I went on to study for my MSc in GIS and Archaeology at the University of Edinburgh in Edinburgh, Scotland. After a year working in Sunnyvale, California, I moved to Urbana, IL to study Geography & GIS at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.
